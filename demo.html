How It Works

Our intelligent system uses a multi-layered approach to optimize traffic flow in real-time.

1

Data Collection

Traffic sensors, cameras, and IoT devices collect real-time data on vehicle counts, speeds, queue lengths, and traffic density across the road network.

2

AI Analysis

Our machine learning models process the collected data to understand traffic patterns, identify congestion points, and predict how traffic will evolve.

3

Optimization Algorithm

The core optimization engine calculates the optimal traffic light timings for each intersection based on current and predicted traffic conditions.

4

Real-time Adjustment

Traffic light controllers receive and implement the optimized timing plans, continuously adjusting to changing conditions.

5

Network Coordination

Adjacent intersections are coordinated to create "green waves" that allow vehicles to move through multiple intersections without stopping.
